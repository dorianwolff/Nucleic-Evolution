About Nucleic Evolution

Nucleic Evolution is a fast-paced game involving both skill and luck, knowledge, strategy, and bluff. Each player selects cards in order to establish their combat zones before engaging in confrontation.

The Lore

Nucleis. A vast universe constantly expanding, with one fundamental principle which differs from ours. The cosmos does not expand by ellacity but by creation. As our Universe simply stretches before its fated retraction, Nucleis creates matter on the border of time and space and will inevitably retract by the opposite of creation; destruction.

With an unlimited supply of matter and creation, planets overflowing with life forms of different degrees of intellect are bound to emerge. Intellect, means a certain understanding of benefits, power and ambition, which eventually lead to warfare. As wars carry on for centuries, even millenia, suddenly, all war ceases. A sudden stop of warfares with grudges amplified over centuries can only happen in two cases; if all opposing tribes are completely eradicated or if a new, bigger, unknown, threat appears. The latter happens.

Planets, full of life forms that were mainly waging wars, collide in the cosmos. Tribes, already used to bloodshed and slaughter, turn their malicious intent to a different direction. The new threat of the unknown. The habitants of planets unite, almost instinctively, and face off against this new threat.

This is the story behind Nucleic Evolution.

Species and Strategies

  • Insects: a control-oriented play-style, based on unit resources to affect combat and confrontation.
  • Dragons: a strategy based on winning to gain extra benefits for latter confrontation.
  • Harpies: playing a game within a game using resources for combat.
  • Androids: strong individually and forcing opponents to play one on one combat.
  • Ancients: the strongest creatures only once unsealed.
  • Angels: winning in one big sweep with strategic positioning.
  • Dinosaurs: nullifying opponent cards and gaining bonuses from doing so.
  • Undead: various benefits from losing and sacrifices.
